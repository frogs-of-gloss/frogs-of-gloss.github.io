November by Marcella Durand

11.20.20

It is like December

and still November,

November is still,

and not still, trees

are not still, sky is

not still and still it

is November: gray

russet month, russeting

unleaving, deleafing,

unfoliating, blowing,

losing, bareness, barely

a tree, only a long branch, dark

against more darkness,

earlier and less light.
